# AI-Powered Virtual Mouse

A Computer Vision-based virtual mouse application that allows you to control your operating system's cursor and execute mouse actions using hand gestures via a webcam.

## Features & Gestures
* **Move Cursor:** Raise only your index finger and move it around.
* **Left Click:** Bring your index and middle fingers close together.
* **Double Click:** Bring your index, middle, and ring fingers close together.
* **Right Click:** Raise your index and middle fingers and spread them wide apart.
* **Drag & Drop:** Pinch your thumb and index finger together to hold; separate them to release.

## Requirements
* Python 3.x
* Webcam
